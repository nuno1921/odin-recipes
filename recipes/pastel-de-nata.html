Pastel de Nata

Prep Time: 1h 20 mins

Cook Time: 8 to 15 mins

Total Time: 1h 30 to 1h 45 min

Servings: 12

Pastel de Nata is one of the most popular convent sweets in Portugal. With a centennial recipe dated 1837, today they are a tourist attraction. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions below.

Pastel de nata:

    Pastry Ingredients:

  • 500g Flour
  • 350ml Water
  • Salt
  • 250g Butter or Margarine

    • Syrup Ingredients:

  • 200g Sugar
  • 175ml Water
  • Cinnamon stick

    • Filling Ingredients:

  • 25g Flour
  • 20g Corn starch
  • 250ml Milk
  • 5 gems
  • Syrup

Step by Step:

    Prepare the pastry:

  1. Place in a bowl the flour, water and salt. With your hands, knead the pastry and knead well all the ingredients until it becomes consistent and soft. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Spread the pastry until it becomes thin. After it, place the butter on top and knead well.
  3. After mixing, spread the pastry on the table and thin it. Then, keep fold it two to three times and let it rest.

    4. Prepare the Syrup:

  4. In a pot, put water,sugar, lemon peel and cinnamon stick. Let it boil without stirring. As soon as it boils, count a minute and turn off the stove.

    5. Prepare the Pastel de Nata:

  5. In a pot place the flour corn starch and milk, mix it until it becomes creamy. Then add the sugar syrup little by little. In a bowl, stir the gems with a little of the cream and mix. When well mixed, add them to the pot and keep stirring. When it's ready store it in a bowl and rest.
  6. Cut pastry rolls in smaller pieces, place them inside the mold.
  7. Shape the pastry to fill the mold.
  8. Fill the pastry with the filling and put them in the oven at the highest temperature possible for 8 to 15 minutes.

Enjoy!
